Why we’re doing this

Taskmaster has been one of our favorite shows to watch together — the perfect mix of creativity, chaos, and laughing until it hurts.

Over the years we’ve even hosted a few “Taskmaster nights” with friends, making up ridiculous challenges and arguing (politely) about points.

So we wanted to bring that same energy into the wedding — a few fun, low-pressure tasks you can do before the day, just for bragging rights (and possibly some prizes or even a golden head).

No pressure to do them all — pick one that feels fun.

If you’ve never seen it, this quick intro explains the vibe.
